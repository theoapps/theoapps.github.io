### boqart (2015)
THE symmetrical box drawing app. Provides a canvas that auto-symmetrizes your brush strokes, which makes it surprisingly easy to create super cool pieces of abstract art. Has functionality for users to save and export their drawings to email/text/instagram/twitter/facebook. Users can also share, download, and rate drawings with each other inside the app. Currently using Apple's Cloudkit as a backend for this. Additionally users can dynamisize their collections with colors, watch them animate, and view them on the companion Apple Watch app.
